# Measuring Volume Of Irregular Objects
### Instruments
- Measuring cylinder
- Water
### Procedure
1. Fill the water in the measuring cylinder
2. Take initial volume (reading)
3. Put object into measuring cylinder

| Group        | A     | B     | C     |
| ------------ | ----- | ----- | ----- |
| Initial (V₁) | 31cm³ | 29cm³ | 30cm³ |
| Final (V₂)   | 36cm³ | 35cm³ | 35cm³ |

>**Group A**
$$V = V_2 - V_1 $$
$$V = 36 - 31$$
$$V = 6cm^3$$
