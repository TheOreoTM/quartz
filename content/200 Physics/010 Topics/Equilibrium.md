1. For the above diagram, find the [[Weight]](w) to bring it to equilibrium
$$C.W moment = Anti.C.W moment$$
$$f \cdot d = f \cdot d$$
$$300 \cdot 1.5 = w \cdot 1$$
$$450 \cdot = w \cdot 1$$
$$w = \frac{450}{1}$$
$$w = 450N$$
